Study Approach

My preparation strategy combined structured learning paths, video courses, practice exams, and hands-on experience with Microsoft 365 tools.

Microsoft Learn Training

I started with the official Microsoft Learn training paths, which provided a solid foundation in cloud concepts, Microsoft 365 services, pricing models, and security features. The practice exam helped me gauge my progress and identify areas for improvement.

John Savill's MS-900 Study Cram 2.0 Video

The John Savill MS-900 Study Cram 2.0 video was incredibly helpful for reinforcing the core concepts. His visual explanations and detailed coverage of key topics helped solidify my understanding.

FreeCodeCamp MS-900 Full Course

The FreeCodeCamp MS-900 Full Course was another excellent resource. It provided an in-depth review of every exam topic, helping me build confidence in my knowledge of Microsoft 365 services and features.

ExamPro Free Practice Exam

I also took the ExamPro Free Practice Exam, which helped highlight additional concepts and provided valuable test-taking experience, ensuring I was fully prepared for the exam format.

LinkedIn Learning MS-900 Cert Prep by Microsoft Press

Finally, the LinkedIn Learning Microsoft 365 Fundamentals (MS-900) Cert Prep by Microsoft Press gave me practical insights into the Microsoft 365 admin tools. The real-world demonstrations of how to navigate and utilize these tools were invaluable during my preparation.

Hands-on Experience

In addition to studying, I gained hands-on experience with Microsoft 365 applications, such as Microsoft Teams, SharePoint, and OneDrive. This helped me understand the practical applications of the cloud services covered in the exam.
