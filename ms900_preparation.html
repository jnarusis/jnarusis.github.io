Preparing for the MS-900 Microsoft 365 Fundamentals Exam

As part of my learning journey, I have been actively preparing for the MS-900 Microsoft 365 Fundamentals exam. This journey has allowed me to dive deep into Microsoft 365 services, cloud concepts, pricing models, security, compliance, and support structures. Below is an overview of my comprehensive study approach and the resources I used to reinforce my understanding.

Study Approach

My preparation was a combination of structured learning paths, video courses, practice exams, and hands-on experience with Microsoft 365 tools.

Microsoft Learn Training

I started with the official Microsoft Learn training paths. These were essential in building a strong foundation in cloud concepts, Microsoft 365 services, pricing and support options, and security features. The modules provided a step-by-step guide to help me thoroughly understand key areas relevant to the exam.

John Savill's MS-900 Study Cram 2.0 Video

Next, I watched the John Savill MS-900 Study Cram 2.0 video. This was an invaluable resource for quick revision, offering concise and focused insights on all exam objectives. I took detailed notes during the video and used them as a quick reference for core concepts.

FreeCodeCamp MS-900 Full Course

The FreeCodeCamp MS-900 Full Course provided another comprehensive view of Microsoft 365 fundamentals. The course thoroughly covered each exam topic and helped reinforce concepts that I had already studied. It served as a solid secondary resource to reinforce my understanding.

Microsoft Learn Practice Tests

To further test my knowledge, I completed the official Microsoft Learn practice tests multiple times. These helped me gauge my readiness for the exam and identify any gaps in my understanding, which I then revisited through the videos and learning paths.

Hands-on Experience

In addition to the theoretical knowledge, I explored the freely available online tools within Microsoft 365 to gain hands-on experience. I experimented with applications such as Microsoft Teams, SharePoint, and OneDrive to understand how they function and align with the cloud-based services covered in the exam.

Next Steps

With the study resources completed and multiple rounds of practice exams behind me, I feel confident in my readiness for the MS-900 exam. My next step is to schedule the exam and continue revising the material until exam day.
